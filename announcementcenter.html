📢 How Announcements Work

To submit an announcement for the PlanetGo community:

  1. Contact BadukAlpha on online-go.com
  2. Provide your announcement content (title, description, relevant links)
  3. Our moderators will review and approve appropriate submissions
  4. Approved announcements will appear in the News section

Note: All announcements are subject to moderation to ensure quality and relevance to the Go community.
